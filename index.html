About the Artist

Amy Richardson

I make stuff out of wood in my East Nashville garage. I spend most weekdays in front of a computer, so woodworking feeds my creative side.

My work focuses primarily on geometric wood mosaics built from individually cut pieces of hardwood. I rarely start with a design in mind and generally let the wood grains and tones dictate where things go. I occasionally incorporate scroll-saw designs against mosaic backdrops. Most pieces go through several iterations before I commit to the glue-down — the goal is simple: create something interesting to look at and worth sharing with friends and family.
